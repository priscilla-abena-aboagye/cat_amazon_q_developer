About Whiskers & Paws

Our Mission

At Whiskers & Paws, we believe every cat deserves a loving home and every family deserves the joy of feline companionship. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats while educating our community about responsible pet ownership.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate cat lovers, Whiskers & Paws began as a small foster network in our local community. What started with just a few volunteers has grown into a dedicated organization that has helped over 500 cats find their forever homes.

We work closely with local veterinarians to ensure all our cats receive proper medical care, including spaying/neutering, vaccinations, and any necessary treatments. Our foster families provide temporary homes where cats can recover, socialize, and prepare for adoption.
